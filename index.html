About Us

Established in year 2011 by a team of professionals who have worked across various domains of IT, Non-IT, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Banking, Finance, and Property Facility Management.

HirePark is a Consultancy firm specializing in the areas of Talent Acquisition & Human Capital Management. We believe in our core competencies in the area of recruitment as we continue to include every functioning from HR Consulting to Employer Branding. HirePark focuses on helping companies recruit and hire the best candidates within the client's budget. HirePark uses a variety of practices for talent searching, including using proprietary databases, reviewing potential internal candidates, searching social media sites such as LinkedIn and posting positions on job boards that specialize in specific industries and professions.

Recruitment Process

  • Step 1: Accreditation
    Accreditation of both HirePark and the Client to seek permission to conduct recruitment in the designated states.
  • Step 2: Sourcing and Verifying
    Sourcing and verifying of all required documents as required by the regulatory bodies of both the sourcing and placing parties.
  • Step 3: Interview Itinerary
    Arrangement of itinerary[Job description, Virtual Conference Call] for the parties involved in the recruitment process.
  • Step 4: Documents Screening
    Initial screening of documents if requested by client.
